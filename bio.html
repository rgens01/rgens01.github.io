// Russell W. Gens //

A Short Biography.
Russell is a hopeful young person looking to make a difference in this world. He is especially compelled by how the way we produce, distribute and consume food contributes to social inequity and environmental harm. By majoring in Computer Science and studying Food Systems, Nutrition & the Environment in the Environmental Studies Department at Tufts, he hopes to stumble across some innovative, people-driven solutions to some of the issues surrounding our food systems.
